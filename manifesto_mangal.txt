==============================================
  MY OPEN SOURCE MANIFESTO
  By: mangal | Date: 29 March 2026
==============================================

  Every day, I rely on Linux — a tool that
  exists because someone chose to build in
  the open and share their work freely.

  To me, freedom means Our decisions not influenced by others. That is
  exactly what the Linux Kernel represents:
  the freedom to see how your computer works,
  change it, and pass that knowledge on.

  Inspired by Linus Torvalds — who shared his
  kernel with the world in 1991 — I too want
  to build IOT helping in Environment Sustainability and release it freely,
  so that anyone, anywhere, can use it,
  improve it, and stand on my shoulders
  just as I stand on the shoulders of those
  who came before me.

  This is the open source promise.
==============================================
